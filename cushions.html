Cushions

  • Cushions

    A cushion is a soft bag of some ornamental material, stuffed with wool, hair, feathers, polyester staple fiber, non-woven material, or even paper torn into fragments. It may be used for sitting or kneeling upon, or to soften the hardness or angularity of a chair or couch. Cotton and linen are widely used in cushion manufacture and both are hard wearing and washable.
